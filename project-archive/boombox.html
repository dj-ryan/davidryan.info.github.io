BOOMBOX

This project was born from a plank of wood that I had from a hight chart that my mother had kept of me. It was taking up space but I still wanted to use if for something memorable. So I turned it into a housing for two car speakers with a bluetooth amplifier to drive them. I added some convinces like a power switch and a handel.

CONSTRUCTION

2019

Finished build

The original board was cut into four pieces and screwed together with 8 counter sunk screws. The construction was satisfactory even though there was a small gap in one of the joints. Two oval holes were cut into the sides of box to allow the speakers to be mounted on the inside. The power supply and bluetooth amplifier are mounted on standoffs on the bottom of the box. A master switch, mounted on the top of the box, cuts current to the power supply. A velcro strap is connected to the top of the mox for ease of carying. There are two diffrent types of speakers in the box. A RockforForsgate and a JPL full range.
In retrospect it is clear that the lack of base in the audio comes from a lack of a enclosure. The back sides of the membranes are exposed to the same air that the front sides are. This is not conducive to a full audio range and is not the environment that these speakers were designed for.
